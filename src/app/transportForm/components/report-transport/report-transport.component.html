Submission

To use this form you must identify for which Health Canada Programme this submission is intended.
You must also have a Case Number assigned by Health Canada.

- Consumer Product Safety Progamme includes cosmetics and consumer products such as toys and clothes, household appliances and furniture, sporting goods, etc.
- Additional submissions to the Food Directorate may include documentation for novel foods, infant formula, or food additives.
- The Health Products Programme includes human and veterinary drugs, biologic and genetic therapies, radio-pharmaceuticals, medical devices, and natural health products.
